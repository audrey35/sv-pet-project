Ann’s Story

Ann’s career includes over 25 years of experience in software design and development. She is a veteran of Oracle where she managed a regional consulting organization. She was also an early employee of several Internet startups, none of which went public. She is currently the co-founder and CEO of a small biotech consulting firm. Ann is a lifelong cat and dog owner and has been involved with cat rescue for several years. In 2015 she co-founded The Dancing Cat, an innovative cat adoption lounge in San Jose.

Ann and her husband Rob are the owners of a rescue dog named Rocket, two semi-feral cats named Coco and Prowler, and a very spoiled indoor cat named Scout. She is proud to be serving on the SVPP Board of Directors.
