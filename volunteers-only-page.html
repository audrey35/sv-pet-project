Welcome to Our Volunteers Only Page!

We are excited to welcome you to our volunteer-only page.

This page exists to provide our volunteers key resources to help Silicon Valley Pet Project with our mission. We strive to create a team of like-minded volunteers that have a shared vision and want to give back to the community, share good stories, have fun and promote continuing education regarding animal rescue opportunities.

Thank you for giving your very valuable time to our organization. Should you have feedback about any of these resources, please contact us at volunteer@svpetproject.org.

Note: Information, data and photos used in any of the training materials on this page are strictly confidential and are supplied with the understanding that they will be held confidentially and not disclosed to third parties without the prior written consent of Silicon Valley Pet Project.
