Are you interested in meeting one of our adoptable pets? Please complete our online application below. Once we have received your online adoption application, we will be in contact with you to arrange a meeting.

Also note that all of our rescue pets are in private foster homes and meetings will be coordinated with foster parents. Typically, after you meet the rescue pet, we will follow up with you to see how the meeting went. If a match is made, we can finalize the adoption quickly. Please note that we may require a home check as part of the application process.

Didn't find what you were looking for? Ask us about our pets in foster care. They may not be listed on our website yet.

Also, if we cannot make a match with one of our adoptable pets, we will be happy to help you find a pet with another reputable rescue organization.

Thank you for supporting adoption.

While adoption fees are not tax-deductible to adopters, they may qualify for your employer’s matching gift program. Please submit your adoption fee payment receipt to your employer on behalf of Silicon Valley Pet Project (Tax ID: 47-2361690) and help us save even more homeless pets!

All pets have been spayed/neutered, vaccinated and microchipped. Adult dogs have been heartworm tested and are on heart worm preventative.

Golden Paws Program (Seniors for Seniors)

The companionship and devotion of a pet is something that brings joy to life. Golden Paws Program is designed to give our older animals the opportunity to be placed in a loving home by matching them with senior citizens who are 65 years +. An older animal would be a great choice for an older person that needs a buddy! Adoption fees are discounted 50%.

Adopt-A-Buddy Program (Buddy Program)

We realize the importance of having a buddy! Adopt a second pet from us at time of adoption (or within a month) and receive a discounted adoption fee. We believe it is emotionally healthy for animals to have a friend and have found that some behavioral issues can easily be solved or never occur with the addition of a second pet into a household. Second Adoption fee is 50% off.

Paw ApplePets for Vets Program

Thank you to all who have served our country! All U.S. Veterans are eligible to adopt a pet at the reduced fee. Adoption fees are discounted 50% with military ID.

Paw AppleFrequent Adopter Program

For our loyal adopters, we are happy to offer a discounted adoption fee if you have adopted from us before. Adoption fees are discounted 25% with copy of a SVPP adoption agreement.
