Buy, Sell, Shop & Walk

eBay Giving Works is a program that enables members of the eBay community to donate to their favorite cause. Sellers can give a portion of the proceeds from their sales, buyers can add a donation to their purchase during checkout, and anyone with a PayPal account can donate to nonprofits right away – without buying or selling anything.
Silicon Valley Pet Project is a charity partner with Fashion Project, whose mission is to change the world one piece of clothing at a time. SVPP will receive up to 55% of net proceeds on the sale of your donated clothes, handbags, shoes and accessories when you sign up. Fashion Project pays to ship your donated items and does all the work! Was there ever a better reason to clean out your closets?
Silicon Valley Pet Project is a charity partner with eScrip. You shop, and merchants give back! Shop online, buy groceries, or dine out to make a difference for SVPP shelter pets. Register your credit cards with eScrip and let the donating begin! Please check individual merchants for accepted forms of payment to earn eScrip rewards.
Every time you shop on Amazon, you can help our organization in our mission! Go to smile.amazon.com and select Silicon Valley Pet Project as your charity of choice. Amazon donates a small percentage of your purchase to our organization. This is the same site as Amazon — no differences in prices or processes.

Vehicle Donations

You can donate any car, truck, RV, motorcycle, or boat – in any condition – to help save animals! Your tax-deductible gift will be used for life-saving services for animals in need.

  1. Fill out this online vehicle donation form. 
  2. Someone will contact you to pick up the vehicle.
  3. Once the car is sold, you will receive a donation receipt will the full tax-deductible sales price for your records

Please let us know if you have any questions.
